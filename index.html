What is Digital Wallet?

Digital Wallet helps university students track their finances.

They can see the total amount of cash in their bank account. They can view their spending by category (e.g.food, utilities, education, transportation) and set monthly spending goals for each class.

Digit Wallet also provides visual feedback on the user's spending activity:

Green = You are adhering to your monthly limit.

Orange = You are close to overpassing your monthly limit.

Red = You have exceeded your monthly limit.
